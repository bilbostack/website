{{<speaker_bio>}}

Carlos Iglesias has been, since 2003, CEO and founding partner of Runroom.
Included by Forbes Spain in the list of the 100 most creative people in the business world, he is an Associate Professor and Academic Director at Esade Executive Education, where he shares his knowledge in innovation, organizational transformation, and business agility.
Since 2018 he has directed and hosted Realworld, the podcast about people-centered innovation.

{{</speaker_bio>}}

{{<speaker_talk title="#NoProduct">}}
For years, we measured the success of product teams by their capacity to deliver: more features, more speed, more releases.
But in the AI era, where building is easier and faster than ever, the real challenge is no longer delivery, but discovery: understanding what's worth building before building it.
#NoProduct is a reflection born from practice, not theory.
From facing the limits of the output mindset and learning, through trial, error and conversation, what happens when a team focuses on impact and learning instead of deliveries.
In this talk I'll share what we've seen work (and what hasn't) when transforming the way product teams work, decide and measure their success.
Because if AI accelerates delivery, the real value is in learning faster, not just building faster.

{{</speaker_talk>}}
