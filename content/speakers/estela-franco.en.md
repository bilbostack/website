{{<speaker_talk title="Greatest Disaster Hits in Web Performance">}}
Turn up the volume on performance! In this session, we'll review greatest hits from yesterday and today that are destroying your project's loading speed, user experience, and business metrics (and how to avoid them!). Among many other bangers, you'll learn to identify JavaScript's killer melodies, the slow ballads of asynchronous loading, and the riffs that block the main thread. Discover how to stop playing the song of failure and turn your project into the headliner of performance.
