Curious by nature, Marta Barrio Marcos has been working in cybersecurity for over 14 years, specializing in offensive security. She is currently focused on Securiters, a project that started as a podcast and has grown into an outreach and training initiative for people who want to enter the field. She also teaches in various security courses and master’s programs such as Cybersecurity Campus, EIP, and ISACA. She holds several technical certifications, including OSCP, OSWP, OSCE, and CRTP, among others.
