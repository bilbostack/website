I’ve been developing software since I was a child… and I hope to keep doing it until I stop being one, someday. Every few years I discover that I have no idea what I’m doing, and I start learning all over again. Along the way, I try to help others learn and build beautiful things.

{{<speaker_talk title="The End of Software">}}
We’ve always said that we were a young industry, that other professions have been evolving and transforming for centuries. That explained many of our problems, but it also gave us hope. Normalization, practices, training, alignment, patterns, communication… everything slowly pushing us toward a bright future for the profession.

And suddenly… everything is over.
